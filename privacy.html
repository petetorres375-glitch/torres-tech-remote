Privacy Policy

Effective: June 30, 2026  ·  Torres Tech Remote  ·  pedro_torres@torrestechremote.com

Plain language summary: We collect only what's needed to run your account. We don't sell your data. We don't store your AI conversations. Your contacts and account info stay in your account until you delete it.
1

Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Name and email address (at registration)
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — we never store your plain-text password)
  • Contact data you enter into Workblox Business (stored per your account)

Information collected automatically:

  • Authentication tokens (stored locally on your device, not on our servers)
  • Content moderation metadata — when our automated safety system flags a request, we log the category of the flag (e.g., "phishing") and a timestamp. We do not log your actual input text.
✓  We do NOT collect: the content of your AI prompts or generated outputs. The Service is stateless — your conversations are not stored on our servers.
2

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • To authenticate your identity and maintain your account
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, notifications)
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and detect abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data to train AI models.

3

Third-Party Services

The Service relies on the following third parties, each with their own privacy practices:

Service Purpose
Anthropic Claude API Processes your AI requests; subject to Anthropic's usage policies and privacy policy
Google OAuth Optional sign-in with Google; Google authenticates you per their privacy policy
SendGrid Delivers transactional emails (account verification, notifications) on our behalf
Railway Hosts our backend servers and database; your data resides on Railway's infrastructure

4

Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Content moderation logs (category + timestamp only, never your input text) are retained for up to 12 months for security analysis, then deleted
5

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and JWT-based authentication. No system is 100% secure. In the event of a breach affecting your data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

6

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise these rights, contact: pedro_torres@torrestechremote.com

7

Cookies and Local Storage

The Service uses browser local storage (not cookies) to maintain your login session on your device. No third-party tracking cookies are used. You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings.

8

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it promptly. Contact us at pedro_torres@torrestechremote.com if you believe this has occurred.

9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy with a revised effective date. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
